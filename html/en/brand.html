KAILAS FUGA Innovating Sports Brand

KAILAS FUGA is a trail running series from KAILAS, a premium mountaineering brand launched in 2003.
It is available in over 1,000 stores worldwide, including Canada, Switzerland, Spain, Japan, Singapore, Malaysia, Russia, Greece, and Vietnam,
and is loved by trail runners around the world for its exceptional quality and high performance.

From beginners taking their first steps in trail running to elite athletes competing at the highest level,
KAILAS FUGA offers a comprehensive lineup designed to suit every runner and every purpose.
Now, experience this globally recognized trail running brand, celebrated for its cutting-edge technology and craftsmanship, here in Korea.
